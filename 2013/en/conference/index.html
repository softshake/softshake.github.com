THE CONFERENCE 2013

After the success of the previous editions, Softshake is back for the 3rd edition. Softshake 2013 will take place on thursday 24th and friday 25th octobre, in Geneva, Switzerland. The conference will be hosted at Haute Ecole du Paysage, d'Ingénierie et d'Architecture de Genève (hepia).

Location details and access plan.

This year we are expecting more than 300 attendees. Sessions will be presented in French and English.

Softshake 2013 is a multi-disciplinary conference offering a varied range of tracks and topics. Sessions will be divided in 5 main tracks:

  • Agile
  • Java
  • Microsoft
  • Mobile
  • Ruby

Also, the Incubator track will contain sessions that does not fit in any other category.

This conference is organized with the help of local developer communities:

  • The group of iPhone developers of romand Switzerland
  • The Geneva JUG
  • Agile Geneva
  • The Ruby Brigade of Geneva
