Call for papers is open. The deadline for session submissions is Sunday, July 14th 2013.

Proceed to registration

Submissions and speakers

  • Sessions are accepted in French, a limited number of sessions in English will be accepted.
  • Every session slot is one hour long. You can propose a session that spans few time slots.
  • The format of the session is free - academic, workshop, interactive - surprise your audience!
  • The speaker agrees to provide a PDF version of support materials. It will be published online and made available to conference attendees.
  • The speaker will have free access to the conference. If a session is presented by two speakers, only the main speaker will get a free pass.
  • Due to tight budget we'll not be covering travel and accomodation expenses of the speakers. Thank you for understanding.

Tracks

This year the conference will is proposing next tracks: Agile, Java, Microsoft, Mobile, Ruby and Incubator. Mobile track will group sessions about any mobile technologies: iOS, Android, PhoneGap. You can also submit a session for Incubator track that will welcome sessions that doesn't clearly fit in any other category.
